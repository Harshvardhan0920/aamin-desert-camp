Welcome to Aamin

Experience the True Essence of the Desert

Welcome to Aamin Desert Camp, where luxury seamlessly blends with the raw, untamed beauty of the Thar Desert. Founded with a deep-rooted passion for preserving traditional Rajasthani hospitality, we offer an unforgettable escape from the chaos of modern city life.

Our camp is thoughtfully designed to provide you with the utmost comfort while keeping you connected to nature. Whether you are seeking a peaceful romantic retreat under the starlit sky or an adventurous safari across the rolling golden dunes, our team curates experiences that linger in your memory forever.
